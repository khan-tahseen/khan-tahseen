### About Me

I'm **Tahseen**, a Software Engineer (SDE-II) based in Delhi, India, with ~4.5 years of experience building robust, scalable applications across web, mobile, and backend.

My day-to-day centers on payment & subscription systems and cross-platform mobile development. Outside of work I ship native iOS/macOS apps and run my own infrastructure end to end.

- 🛠️ &nbsp;**Currently:** designing payment + subscription infrastructure — Razorpay, PhonePe, Stripe, Apple IAP & Google Play — with durable webhook ingestion and reconciliation on AWS (Lambda · SQS · SES)
- 🚀 &nbsp;**Recently shipped:** *Slope* (SwiftUI fat-loss & weight-trend tracker) and *Expences* (native expense tracker for iOS / iPadOS / macOS)
- 🧱 &nbsp;**Self-hosted:** all personal projects on a Hetzner VPS — Nginx · Docker · Certbot
- 🎓 &nbsp;**Background:** M.Sc. Mathematics — I like problems with structure
